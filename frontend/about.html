About Us

Welcome to Bridge The Gap, a platform designed to help guitar players discover their next song to learn with ease.

As a guitarist myself, I often found it difficult to figure out which songs to tackle next. This challenge led me to create Bridge The Gap, a resource that takes the guesswork out of song selection.
